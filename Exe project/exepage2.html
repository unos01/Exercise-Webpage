Exercise Page

Full-body workouts target multiple major muscle groups in a single workout session. Practice a mix of isolation and full-body exercises to maximize your workout, build lean muscle mass, and improve your overall health.

Push-ups: Push-ups activate your pecs, triceps, and anterior deltoids.

Knee crunches: The knee crunch is a bodyweight exercise that helps build core strength and targets your upper abdominal muscles.

Lat pulldown: A lat pulldown is a compound exercise that targets your arms and back muscles.

Bench press: The barbell bench press is an upper-body exercise that can help build chest muscles (pecs) and shoulders (delts). The lower back, core, and glutes stabilize the lower body.

Pull-ups: Pull-ups are an upper-body bodyweight workout that activates your upper back and biceps, including your rhomboids, triceps, and delts. Pull-ups can also help build your grip strength.
